The Ancient

Rajasthani civilization

The historic and ancient civilization of Rajasthan is a rich tapestry of cultures and kingdoms that have flourished over millennia. The region has been home to various civilizations, and has witnessed the rise and fall of several dynasties.

Ancient History

Rajasthan was part of the Indus Valley civilization, associated with the Ahar-Banas and Ochre-Coloured Pottery cultures. The timeline of Rajasthan's history spans from the development of the Sonthi culture (4600 BC) to the emergence of Rajput clans (700 AD).

Medival & Modern Period

The medieval period saw the rise of Rajput clans, such as the Chauhans, Guhilas, and Rathors. The region also witnessed the rule of various dynasties, including the Mauryas, Guptas, and Huns.

Rajputs & Warrior Clans

The 12th century marked a significant period in Rajasthan's history with the emergence of Rajput kingdoms, who played a significant role in shaping the region's history through their valor and chivalry.
